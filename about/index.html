About Andy

Jan 1, 0001

Hi! I’m Andy. I am a petrophysicist / data scientist within the oil in gas industry and based in Aberdeen, Scotland. I have over 15 years experience petrophysics, log analysis and geology. Over the years I have developed my skills in Python and continuing to learn everyday.

When I am not doing my day job I can be found out with the camera photographing nature, catching up on the latest SpaceX launches or learning about productivity and Notion.
